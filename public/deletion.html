Data Deletion Instructions

Request Deletion of Your Data

If you want to delete your account and all associated data from Azimut Kosher Kravi, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Send an email to our support team using the contact information below
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide the email address associated with your account
  4. Specify which Facebook account you used to log in (if applicable)

Contact Information

Email us at:

zvimarmor@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information
  • Chat history and messages
  • Workout preferences and history
  • Any personal data associated with your Facebook login

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of receiving your request. We will send you a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted.

Important Note:

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you will need to create a new account if you wish to use our services again.

Alternative: Deactivate Your Account

If you prefer to temporarily deactivate your account instead of permanent deletion, please mention this in your email request. Deactivated accounts can be reactivated by contacting support.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please don't hesitate to contact us at zvimarmor@gmail.com
